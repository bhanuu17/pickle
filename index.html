Our Story

Leelakrishna Pickles began in our grandmother’s kitchen, where her homemade pickles were the heart of every meal. Made with love, traditional recipes, and sun-dried spices, her pickles became everyone’s favorite. Inspired by her legacy, we started this brand to share that same authentic taste—pure, handmade, and full of flavor—with every home.

Our Process

# At Leelakrishna Pickles, every jar begins with the finest handpicked vegetables and spices sourced from trusted farms.
# We follow a traditional homemade process, starting with thorough cleaning and natural sun-drying to preserve freshness.
# Our spices are freshly ground in-house and blended with cold-pressed oils like sesame or mustard to enhance both taste and health.
# The mixture is then marinated in traditional ceramic jars under the sun, allowing the flavors to mature naturally without any artificial preservatives or vinegar.
# Each pickle is hand-filled with care and packed hygienically, delivering the authentic taste of Andhra homemade pickles—rich, spicy, and full of love, just like how grandma made it.
